Detailed Description

Registration class registers Teams and Athletes.

DEFINTION: The process of obtaining athletes who have successfully completed all qualification rounds prior to the Winter Olympics. Registration places athletes into their respective team and places athletes to a specified event.

CONSTRAINTS: Sets a maximum limit of athletes per event. Sets a max limit on number of teams in the Winter Olympics.
