Detailed Description

PairSkating class is one of the 3 FigureSkating events.

DEFINITION: Form of figure skating where athletes participate in male/female pairs. The utilization of long program style where each athlete is free to do their own unique performance complete with technical tricks is present.

CONSTRAINTS: The pairs are male female. Each pair performs solo on the rink at a given time. Total duration of performance is 4 mintues and 30 seconds.
