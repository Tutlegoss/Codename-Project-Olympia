Detailed Description

Event class is the superclass of all the 2 event types and 6 specific events.

DEFINITION: The specific competition at a specified rink determined by the schedule.

CONSTRAINTS: Limited to the six official events of the Winter Olympics. Events will not disturb those who are not registered for that specific event. Expunges the highest and lowest scores an individual or pair receives. Total score is the average of the remaining judges scores. Timed events utilize time as the score which a judge verifies.
