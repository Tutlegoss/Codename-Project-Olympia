Detailed Description

FigureSkating class is the superclass of the 3 FigureSkating events.

DEFINITION: Skating done on ice in synchronization with music that is scored based upon technical tricks and or the synchronization of each athlete in a pair. One of the two main sporting categories that consist of three unique events which athletes participate separately as well as in pairs.

CONSTRAINTS: None
